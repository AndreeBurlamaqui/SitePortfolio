This research compiles "Good Practices" gathered from 29 post-mortems and advisory videos for indie/solo developers. It synthesizes community knowledge into key pillars: efficient production pipelines, zero-budget marketing, design iteration, and maintaining mental health to avoid burnout.

> <small>It was written in PT-BR at the same time as the thesis project was being developed.</small> <br>
> <small>Later translated to ENG and published by Acta Ludologica Volume 4 in 2021.</small>
